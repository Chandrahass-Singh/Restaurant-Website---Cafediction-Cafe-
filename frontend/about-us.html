About Us

We at Cafediction Cafe always try to source local and organic ingredients wherever possible.

  • Home
  • About us

We at Cafediction Cafe always try to source local and organic ingredients wherever possible. In addition to selecting the best and premium ingredients and cooking everything from scratch with love, we constantly try to improve our food quality, seeking your cooperation in that endeavour. Your comfort is our priority and we only strive to deliver the most wholesome and flavourful dining options for you and your loved ones.

We make sure to reduce any negative impact on the environment by using environmentally friendly methods of sourcing, preparing and packaging and also adhering to all the government implemented rules and regulations. Our promise to you is to always provide you with the most authentic and delicious dishes, with world-class service and ambience, making your dining experience memorable.
